About m(ux)

m(ux) is a music-centric event, intended as a safe haven where no genre or medium is left behind. Our goal is to let artists perform not only their iconic, smashing hits, but also their most personal, deep-in-the-vault favorites. Meet Boston’s first dedicated space for alt-electronic elements – anything and everything on the decks.

Expect curated collectives of DJs, artists, and creators of all formats to have their expression felt and cherished.

Anticipate discoveries of genres never before heard.

Allow your soul to flow in any direction.
